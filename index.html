About Him!

Meet Mylo! 🐾

In 2023, our family was lucky enough to welcome a 2-year-old Mini Poodle named Mylo, who we adopted from Sunny Meadows Farm in Philly. He’s a bit shy around new faces, but once he’s comfortable, he’s all energy and affection. Mylo absolutely thrives with his family and becomes the center of attention with his playful personality.

One of his favorite activities is zooming around the house, especially when there’s a stuffed animal in sight. It’s like a game for him, though we’ve learned that the stuffed toys rarely survive his enthusiastic play. But honestly, watching him go after them with such excitement is one of the highlights of our day.

When he’s not playing, Mylo is all about cuddling and being the center of attention. He’s an absolute cuddle machine, always seeking out a lap or a snuggle buddy. But fair warning—if you’re in the same room as him, be prepared for a barrage of kisses, because Mylo is definitely a licking enthusiast.

Overall, Mylo is the kind of dog who makes everything better. His goofy antics, endless love, and infectious energy bring joy to our home every single day, and we can’t imagine life without him.
